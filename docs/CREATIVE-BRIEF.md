## Creative Strategy Overview

Architecture and interior design is a **visual, high-trust, high-ticket** category.

Creative must do three jobs simultaneously:
1. **Inspire** — show the quality of work that the studio produces
2. **Qualify** — signal that this is for serious homeowners with real projects
3. **Reduce friction** — make the quiz feel easy and non-committal

**Core creative principle:** Let the portfolio do the heavy lifting. The ad is a window into the studio's best work. The copy reduces anxiety and explains what happens next.

## Platform-Specific Creative Specs

### Google Ads

**Responsive Search Ads (RSA):**
- 10–15 headlines (30 chars each)
- 4 descriptions (90 chars each)
- Target "Excellent" Ad Strength

**Performance Max Asset Groups:**
- Images: 20 total (mix of 1.91:1, 1:1, 4:5)
- Logos: 5 (1:1 + 4:1 landscape)
- Videos: 5 (16:9 landscape minimum; add 9:16 vertical)
- Headlines: 5 (30 chars) + 5 Long Headlines (90 chars)
- Descriptions: 5 (90 chars)

### Meta Ads

| Format | Ratio | Resolution | Duration | Safe Zone |
|--------|-------|-----------|----------|-----------|
| Feed Image | 4:5 | 1080×1350 | — | Full bleed |
| Feed Video | 4:5 | 1080×1350 | 15–30s rec | Full bleed |
| Stories | 9:16 | 1080×1920 | Up to 120s | Centre 1080×1300, above bottom 35% |
| Reels | 9:16 | 1080×1920 | 15–30s rec | Centre 1080×1300, above bottom 35% |
| Carousel | 1:1 | 1080×1080 | — | Full bleed |

**Text limits:**
- Primary text: 125 chars recommended (2200 max)
- Headline: 40 chars
- CTA: Use "Learn More" or "Get Quote"

### X (Twitter) Ads

| Format | Ratio | Resolution | Notes |
|--------|-------|-----------|-------|
| Single Image | 16:9 or 1:1 | 1200×675 or 1080×1080 | PNG/JPG, max 5MB |
| Video | 16:9 or 1:1 | 1920×1080 or 1080×1080 | MP4, max 1GB, 15–30s rec |
| GIF/animated | 1:1 | 800×800 min | Max 15MB |

**Copy length:** 280 chars max tweet, aim for 200 or under for clarity

## Creative Concepts — Ready to Brief

### Concept 1: "The Portfolio Window" (Static Image)
**Best for:** Meta Feed, Google PMax
```
Visual:    Full-bleed project photo (kitchen extension, living room, etc.)
           Subtle nvelope.co logo bottom-right corner
           Studio name as a small caption: "via Manolo Design Studio"
Headline:  "This could be your home."
Body:      "Browse [Studio Name]'s portfolio and start your project brief.
           Takes 5 minutes."
CTA:       "See Their Work"
```

### Concept 2: "The Quiz Hook" (Video or GIF, 10–15s)
**Best for:** Meta Reels, X, Stories
```
0–2s:   Text on screen: "What kind of architect do you need?" (hook)
2–5s:   Quick flashes of 3 different project styles (residential/commercial/interior)
5–10s:  Screen recording of quiz interface — 3 quick questions
10–15s: Project photo result + "Here's who we matched you with →"
CTA:    "Take the quiz"
Sound:  Upbeat minimal music; subtitles required
```

### Concept 3: "The Before/After" (Carousel or Split-screen)
**Best for:** Meta Carousel, X video
```
Frame 1:  "Before" — empty room or old kitchen
Frame 2:  "After" — completed design by the studio
Frame 3:  Studio name, project summary
Frame 4:  "Your home could be next. Find your architect →"
```

### Concept 4: "The Credibility List" (X native text post)
**Best for:** X promoted posts
```
Tweet:
"What to look for in an architect:

✓ Portfolio of similar projects
✓ Clear communication from day 1
✓ Experience with your local planning office
✓ Fixed fees, not surprises

Find one who ticks every box → nvelope.co"
```

### Concept 5: "The Proof" (Testimonial video, UGC style)
**Best for:** Meta Feed, X
```
Format:   Talking head, phone-shot, authentic feel
Script:   "We tried to find an architect for months. Then we found [Studio]
          on nvelope — submitted our brief on a Tuesday, had a call by Friday.
          We've just broken ground."
CTA:      "Find your studio today"
```

## Creative Testing Plan

### Month 1 — Baseline
Test 1 creative angle per platform. Goal: establish baseline CTR and CVR.

| Platform | Creative A | Creative B |
|----------|-----------|-----------|
| Google | RSA with portfolio-led copy | RSA with quiz-led copy |
| Meta | Static portfolio image | Short video quiz demo |
| X | Text-only thought post | Image with headline |

### Month 2 — Iterate
Kill bottom 50% of creative by CTR and CPL. Double down on winners.

### Month 3 — Scale Winners + New Test
Produce 3 new variants of winning concept. Test before/after concept.

### Fatigue Rules
- **Meta:** Refresh creative every 3–4 weeks for prospecting (frequency >3.0 = fatigue)
- **X:** Refresh every 2–3 weeks (smaller audiences exhaust faster)
- **Google RSA:** Pause any asset marked "Low" for 30+ days; replace with new copy

- [ ] 10–20 high-res project photos (JPG/PNG, min 1200px wide)

- [ ] Before/after pairs (if available)

- [ ] Studio logo (SVG or PNG transparent background)
- [ ] Brand colours (hex codes)
